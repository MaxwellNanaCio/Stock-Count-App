# Stock-Count-App

### Stock Count - OGH
The Stock Count - OGH app is a comprehensive stock reconciliation solution that I developed using Microsoft PowerApps. This app empowers users to perform accurate stock counts at warehouses, with seamless integration with SAP data.

Key Features:
SAP Data Integration: The app seamlessly integrates with SAP, allowing users to access and view real-time stock information for their warehouses. This ensures that the stock count data is always up-to-date and aligned with the enterprise-level inventory management system.

Manual Stock Count: For stock or materials that are not tracked in SAP, the app provides a manual stock count feature. Users can easily input the physical count of these items, enabling a complete and accurate stock reconciliation process.

Intuitive User Interface: The app features a clean and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to navigate and perform stock count tasks. The design prioritizes efficiency and streamlines the overall stock count workflow.

Barcode Scanning Integration: To further enhance the stock count process, the app integrates with barcode scanning capabilities. Users can quickly and accurately capture item details by scanning barcodes, reducing the risk of manual data entry errors.

Reporting and Analytics: The Stock Count - OGH app includes comprehensive reporting and analytics features. Users can generate detailed reports on stock discrepancies, inventory levels, and other key metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making and improved inventory management.

Offline Functionality: The app is designed to work seamlessly in both online and offline environments. Users can perform stock counts even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity, and the data will be synchronized once the connection is restored.

Secure and Scalable: The app is built on the secure and scalable Microsoft PowerApps platform, ensuring data privacy and compliance with organizational policies. It can be easily deployed and scaled across multiple warehouses or locations as needed.

The Stock Count - OGH app has been instrumental in streamlining the stock reconciliation process for our organization. By providing a user-friendly and efficient solution, we have been able to improve inventory accuracy, reduce stock discrepancies, and enhance overall operational efficiency.
